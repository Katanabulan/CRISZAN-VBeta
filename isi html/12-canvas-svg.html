Canvas & SVG

Canvas untuk gambar bitmap via JS; SVG untuk grafis vektor inline.

Contoh Kode

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html><head><meta charset="utf-8"><title>Canvas & SVG</title></head>
<body>
  <canvas id="c" width="200" height="100" style="border:1px solid #000"></canvas>
  <script>var c=document.getElementById('c').getContext('2d');c.fillStyle='red';c.fillRect(10,10,100,50);</script>
  <svg width="200" height="100"><rect x="10" y="10" width="100" height="50" fill="blue"/></svg>
</body></html>
