Formulir HTML

Membuat form dengan input, textarea, dan tombol submit.

Contoh Kode

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html><head><meta charset="utf-8"><title>Form</title></head>
<body>
  <form>
    <label>Nama: <input type="text" name="nama"></label><br><br>
    <label>Email: <input type="email" name="email"></label><br><br>
    <button type="submit">Kirim</button>
  </form>
</body></html>
